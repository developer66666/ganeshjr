My name is Ganesh, and I am currently working as a Software Engineer at Swatantra Systems Pvt Ltd. I have around 4.8 years of experience specializing in SQL and PL/SQL development and support

Currently, I am working on an Enterprise Banking Workflow Automation and Data Integration project, where I am actively involved in the Inventory and Accounts modules. My primary responsibilities include handling change requests (CRs) and work requests (WRs).

I have designed and developed PL/SQL procedures, functions, packages, and triggers for financial workflows, including Current Account (CA), Cash & Banking (CB), Accounts Payable (AP), and Accounts Receivable (AR).

On a daily basis, we start with a stand-up meeting from 10:30 AM to 11:00 AM, where we discuss our progress and new requirements. My manager assigns tasks based on work requests and change requests, and I prepare process specification documents before proceeding with development.

Once the development is complete, I prepare a code review document consisting of the code script and application flow screenshots.
